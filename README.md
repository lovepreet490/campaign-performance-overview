📊 Campaign Performance & Funnel Analytics Dashboard

Most dashboards show numbers.
This project focuses on showing what actually impacts business growth.

Built an end-to-end Marketing Analytics Dashboard using Power BI, SQL, and Python to analyze how users move through the funnel — from impressions to activations — and how different campaigns influence retention, revenue, and ROI.

The goal was not just visualization.
The goal was to understand:

Which channels bring quality users
Where conversion drop-offs happen
Which campaigns generate real business value
How retention changes across cohorts
Where marketing spend becomes inefficient

📈 Key Insights
High traffic does not always mean high-quality users.
Some low-volume channels showed better activation quality and retention.
Funnel leakage after signup had a major impact on overall conversions.
Retention dropped significantly after Day 7 in multiple cohorts.
ROI varied heavily between campaigns despite similar traffic volumes.
